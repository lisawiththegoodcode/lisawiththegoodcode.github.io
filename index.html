Lisa Umhey

Web Developer

Hello, I'm Lisa. I am a student at Austin Coding Academy. I am currently learning the skills it takes to become a professional full-stack developer by night, while working as a florist by day. As a florist, I am empowered to be creative, meticulous, knowledgable, and collaborative to make beautiful things. And I find so much satisfaction in this. My imagination loves this work. But I also feel like something's missing. You see, at my core, while I'm highly imaginative and inclined to create, I'm also an eager learner and relentless problem-solver. And I wish I had more opportunity to leverage these strengths on a daily basis.

After conversations with a developer friend, I decided to give coding a try. I worked through online tutorial after online tutorial with glee; this way of thinking felt like a glove for my brain. So, I enrolled in a professional program to help me make the shift from florist to developer, and here I am, back in school and loving every minute of it. I will graduate in December 2018, and my goal is to attain a full-time position as junior-level developer after graduation. In the meantime, I would love to intern with your team or provide freelace support. Let's collaborate!
