floralist

Floralist is a full-stack web application for floral designers. With one fist full of dirt and the other hand submerged in a bucket of water, floral designers are challenged to find the right time to create and send beautiful visual proposals that win new clients over. This app is a one-stop shop for florists to sort through their own personal image library, craft a custom proposal, and email it to the client. Built with C#, ASP.NET Core MVC, SQL Server, SendGrid API, and in collaboration with a developer colleague. Deployed via Azure.

> check it out > learn more
After logging in, users are greeted with a personal landing page that carousels through their images and urges them to complete any unfinished proposals.
Users can upload, tag, and delete images in their image library. Tags can be chosen from the master list or created and saved.
From My Proposals, users can visually notice the proposals that need completion by their color and tag. Proposals can also be deleted, viewed or emailed from this screen.
When creating a proposal, a user will have a drop-down of their customers, text-areas to customize title and description, and an option to add images.
As users are adding images to their proposals, they may search by tag to find the right fit. In this screen-shot, the user is searching their library by "altar".
Once the proposal is complete, the user can send it from floralist with the click of a button.
